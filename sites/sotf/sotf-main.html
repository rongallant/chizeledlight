Survival of the Fittest is the most in-depth wilderness survival school around. It provides the knowledge and experience needed to survive indefinitely in the wilderness in all types of terrain, climate, and weather. Whether it's your first time out of your backyard or you're an experienced woodsperson, this course is for you.

So join others in one of our action filled courses and learn how to survive.

Our Mission

To provide you, the customer, with the knowledge and experience you need to enjoy the wilderness to its fullest, while at the same time giving you a relaxing and fun filled break from the everyday.

Company Profile

As a former member of the Royal Canadian Regiment (Canadian Infantry) and having many years of experience in wilderness survival, you are guaranteed to learn everything you need to know about wilderness survival. With supplied equipment, all you have to bring is a sense of humor and a willingness to learn.

As the most in-depth school of its kind in Newfoundland, our course has been labeled as being a "top quality training experience that nobody should venture outside their door without".
