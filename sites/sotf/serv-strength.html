One of the most important fitness requirements for wilderness training is strength. It is of vital importance that you be able to carry all the equipment and supplies that will be needed in the wilderness. This course will teach you the benefits of strength training, training suggestions, basic laws of strength training, as well as some simple exercises that will increase different muscles in your body, and a suggested twelve-week strength-training program for the low fit and beginning exerciser.
