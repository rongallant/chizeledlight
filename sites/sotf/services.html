In this course, you will learn how to survive in all types of climate, terrain, and weather. Covering all conditions of summer, winter, and arctic, you will learn everything you need to survive in the wilderness.

Some topics covered are:

  1. Survival Skills
  2. Supplies
  3. Clothing required
  4. Food
  5. Cooking
  6. Basic Knots
  7. Tent and Camp Routine
  8. Shelters
  9. Morale
  10. Tracking
  11. Navigation
  12. What to do when lost
  13. Types of Ice
