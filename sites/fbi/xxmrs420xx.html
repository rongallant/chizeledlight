A self examination


It was a crisp clear night out.  My family and I were sitting outside, watching the fireworks above our heads.  My brother was only 3 then, and when he cried we shut it out because we were so used to it.  But that night his cries could have saved us, if we had only paid attention.

We thought it was just a regular temper tantrum, but when his cries suddenly stopped, we turned around, but it was to late.  They were huddled over him, ripping his limbs one by one off his small, fragile body.  At the sound of our horrified gasps, the creatures turned around, and lunged at us.  The largest of the creatures ran for my father, tackling him, which broke his neck, and killed him instantly.  The smallest of proceded to chase my mom.  Once he caught up with her, he grabbed her feet, and pulled her into his mouth, small bit, by small bit.

I was left to watch my family struggling.  As soon as the shock finally reached my gaping mouth, I let out a horrifying scream, which made all the creatures turn in my direction.  Afraid of what might happen, I just stood still, perfectly still.  The creatures slowly moved close to me, and I stood my ground, not showing my fear.  They finally reached me, and each held out an arm, which had long slinky fingers on the ends.  They seemed so gentle, and drastically different from before.  They took me deep into the forest, down a long trail which led to a large disk looking aircraft.

OK, basically where I want to go with this long story is that, they crown me queen, and create new parents for me, because I was so sad without them.  Then they taught me all kinds of ninja skills, and to master weapons stuff, and then they like took me back home, where I solved like crimes for innocent kids, so the FBI named me top agent at the age of 15...
