What is the Pickyourbrainetc Network?

The Pickyourbrainetc Network is the fastest growing group of talented artists across the nation and abroad. We are united for the common good of the individual artist in order to better sell, maintain and expose artists and their work for all to see.

How do we plan to accomplish this goal?

The Pickyourbrainetc Network has the best exposure strategy for artists around - we are taking no chances on becoming the success story you want to be a part of. We have comprised a 15 member panel who looks at every potential applicant that comes to the network.Fifteen of your peers,artists from every genre will look, critique and then judge new membership. We do this because there are a vast amount of people in this world who consider themselves artists but unfortunately are not. In addition, we are currently compiling a catalogue with all of our artists so that we don’t have to sell over the Internet. We know that there is no way to show absolutely everyone that comes to us on the Internet, but we intend to show each and everyone of our artists on our catalogue. Our Internet site(s) are being put together by some of the most talented freelance web artists we could find, and unlike other companies who just stick their logo on the web site and call it a day, you ( meaning any of you ) who contribute to making the site the best it can be, will be fully credited for your work.

Our catalogues are fun to look at, they show you and who you are at your best, there isn’t a person around who won’t look at them and say -wow this guy is good, even if they don’t decide to purchase from us.

And finally - the all important salesperson, those people who go out there and tell people about you, show them the catalogue and direct them to the site containing your work. These are our three methods of how we are going to make you a resounding success,.. in a nutshell.

What Type of art are we looking for?

What we are looking for is marketable art, we currently have people from all different genres, from Oil or watercolor paintings, to quilting and digital artists. We range, don’t exclude yourself from the possibilities there is a place for most everyone. We look at all works and look at each with a different perspective, we have seen people say they aren’t good enough - let us decide for you whether or not it will sell.

Are we imposing an age limitation?

I would say no, however, there are certain things we look for in especially young people. People who are 16-18 must realize this is a business designed on contract work, if they cannot provide the time and energy it takes to complete any given task, they shall be removed from the network. We don’t mean to say that all people within this age group have not lived up to this expectation, but in a business focused on time and quality of art we cannot rely on those people who cannot perform when necessary. For people of other age, ethnicity, background, etc. It doesn’t matter, we only ask that you derive from your personal experience how long it takes you to do a piece and how much you will regularly charge.

What does it cost you to get into this network?

It costs you nothing to get into this network. This is a scam you are thinking - a scam to take my art away and sell it and leave me in the cold, I get that response a lot, people can’t believe in a company that doesn’t want to take their money right from the start.Selling your art is how we turn a profit, if you are more interested read the information packet provided on this site and you will see what each of the Network artists receives every month, you will also see what it is we do, and the changes we implement and how they effect you. I myself am an artists, a cartoonist by nature and have done various works, you may not have seen them, you might have, I am more than willing to share my experiences with you.

How do we pay for your promotion?

Salespeople who sell your work, work on commission. The catalogue will feature only those companies which cater to art as far as advertisements, making it both good for the advertiser and good for the network to find what is we are looking for. How do we pay for the Internet site? The plan is to get free sites for the time being, so no one has to pay for anything, you will see the professional look of them all together when we are finished with the construction of these sites. Why else should we charge you? To make money for our pockets? The reality is, not many people can make it in this world as a solo artist, and those that do, well.. they don’t have much money to spend on the hopes that they would get sold, otherwise they would contact an agent, we at the Pickyourbrainetc Network know that by uniting us a whole, even if someone comes to the sites to see another artist, they may come across your work, and what’s that really worth to you?

What do we need from you?

We haven’t asked for much, but we will ask for two things. We need you artists Bio., and in that Bio. We will need to have information about you, and who you are, we also need your FULL NAME and SCREEN NAME where applicable, an e-mail address where we can send you pertinent information regarding the network and if you have it.. we need to have a pricing schedule as to what you charge for what you provide and if you are doing business as your name or other. If you like it would be helpful for you to tell us what other work you have done for money, and with whom ( past business, happy past clients )

The other of the two and most importantly your samples. We understand that some of you do not have a scanner or means at your immediate disposal to get your work scanned, if you are interested and that is the case, you must send us an e-mail to tmendez@poweredbyart.com telling us when you can have us that material, at that point your information will be stored in a section with others to be contacted later, if you don’t contact us by your predetermined time frame, we will not contact you, and your file will be deleted.

For those of you who do have the requested samples and Bio. On hand we ask that you send them to the same e-mail address in jpeg format in a zip file. We cannot use large files, there are a vast amount of people trying to get into the network, we have limited space on the web and computers, the smaller the file ( without jeopardizing the content ) the better.
