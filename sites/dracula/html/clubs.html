Clubs and Societies



The following clubs and societies are recommended for Dracula fans and scholars. They are

directed at those who are interested in the Dracula phenomenon either through literature, folklore, movies, or cultural studies. Only clubs and societies about which I have a direct knowledge are

listed here.

--------------------------------------

TRANSYLVANIAN SOCIETY OF DRACULA

The TSD is based in Romania but has chapters in several countries. It is a cultural-historical society of scholars, researchers, and others interested in the Dracula of fiction, folklore and history. For detailed information, click here

--------------------------------------

THE DRACULA SOCIETY

There is a Dracula Society in Great Britain. Though its main activities

are confined to the London area, interested people around the world may

join. For information, visit The Dracula Society

-------------------------------------

THE COUNT DRACULA FAN CLUB

President is Jeanne Youngson. The Club issues regular newsletters and

bulletins, and provides occasional items for purchase. It is one of the

co-sponsors of DRACULA 97.

THE LORD RUTHVEN ASSEMBLY

This group comprises members of the International Association of the Fantastic in the Arts who have a special interest in Dracula/vampire studies. It meets annually at the IAFA's conference in March in Ft. Lauderdale, and issues newsletters in the intervening period. Current president is Elizabeth Miller, Past-President Raymond McNally, Vice-President Margaret Carter.

For more information, contact Stephanie Moss
