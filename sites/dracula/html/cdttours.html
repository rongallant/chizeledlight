DRACULA TOURS IN ROMANIA

The TRANSYLVANIAN SOCIETY OF DRACULA (Romania) offers a variety of Dracula tours that explore locations connected with both the historical Vlad Dracula and the fictional/mythological Count Dracula. Tour operator is The Company of Mysterious Journeys, Bucharest.

Here is a sample of what the TSD is offering:

SOLAR ECLIPSE: August 11, 1999

Watch the full solar eclipse from the top of the ruins of the fortress of Vlad the Impaler at Poenari. Spend overnight in the village of Aref, where legends of Vlad are still told around the bonfire. Combine this (optional) with other tours.



HALLOWEEN 1999 in Transylvania

Visit Sighisoara, birthplace of Vlad the Impaler, and join in the Halloween festivities. Witness the crowning of Miss Transylvania and her investiture as Countess Dracula.



CLASSIC DRACULA TOUR

7 days/8 nights. This tour will include: Bucharest, Sinaia (Royal Palace), Targoviste (Vlad's Wallachian capital), Poenari (Vlad's fortress), Aref (village where legends of Vlad are still told around a bonfire), Brasov, Bran Castle, Sighisoara (Vlad's birthplace), Bistrita and the Borgo Pass (scenes from Stoker's novel).

Other variations are also available.
