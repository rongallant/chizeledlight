We invite manuscripts of scholarly articles on any of the following: Bram Stoker; the novel Dracula; the historical Dracula; the vampire in folklore, fiction, film, popular culture; and related topics. Submissions should be sent on disk with word-processor identified. Printed copy must also be sent. Do not send your manuscript as an e-mail attachment unless it is in a format that can be read by WordPerfect 6. Please follow the MLA style. Contributors are responsible for obtaining any necessary permissions and ensuring observance of copyright. Manuscripts will be vetted by at least two members of the Editorial Board.

The Journal of Dracula Studies is published annually by the Canadian Chapter, Transylvanian Society of Dracula. Copies are provided free to Chapter members as part of their membership benefits. Others may purchase individual copies of the Journal by sending a cheque, bank draft or money order (no credit cards) for $10 (US or Canadian), payable to "Canadian Chapter, TSD". This amount includes airmail postage and handling. For the cost of multiple copies, contact the Editor at [emiller@plato.ucs.mun.ca].
