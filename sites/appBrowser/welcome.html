📖 What is AppBrowser?

AppBrowser is your personal time machine to the early days of the Internet! This revolutionary application simulates the authentic Netscape Navigator experience from 1994, allowing you to browse a curated collection of vintage websites exactly as they would have appeared during the dawn of the World Wide Web.

🎯 How It Works

  • 🖥️ Authentic Interface: Experience the classic Netscape Navigator 3.0 browser interface with retro buttons, menus, and status bars
  • 📁 Site Collection: Browse through our extensive archive of vintage websites from the mid-1990s era
  • 🌍 Fake Domains: Each site loads with its own simulated domain name for that authentic feel
  • ⚡ Instant Navigation: Click on any site in the sidebar to instantly load it in the browser window
  • 🔄 Classic Controls: Use the familiar Back, Forward, Home, and Reload buttons (just like the good old days!)

🚀 Getting Started

Ready to begin your journey through Web history? It's simple! Just look at the sidebar on the left and click on any website that catches your interest. Each site has been carefully preserved to maintain its original 1990s charm, complete with vintage HTML styling, classic color schemes, and those beloved animated GIFs!
