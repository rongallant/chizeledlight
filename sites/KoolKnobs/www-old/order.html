Order your Koolknob

How do I get one?

I'll need some information about your vehicle and the Koolknobs brand product you'd like to have made:

1) What color would you like for the background?

You can choose from red, blue, black, bone, yellow, orange, green, grey, and pearled clear. I can swirl them together for you (two colors) if you like. Or, you can send me a small liquid sample of your paint for a knob color matched to your vehicle!

2) I need to know the thread size of your shift lever.

You can get that easily by just taking your old knob to a hardware store, find a bolt it fits correctly, and note the thread size. It'll be something like 3/8-24 or M10 X 1.5. I can take it from there. You can also email me with the year and make of your vehicle. I've been keeping track of the various vehicles, so I may already have that info.

Some vehicles don't have a threaded shift lever (you Ford truck owners know what I'm talking about here!), but that won't stop us! I'll just need a diameter of the lever (taken with an accurate tool like a caliper) and the length of the ribbed part of the shift lever. Again, if you have questions, email me. I've been keeping track of those, too.

If you have a column shift, I'll need similar information about the ribbed part of the shift lever so I can make you a sleeve that fits over your old knob's location.

3) What shape would you like?

  • 2-1/4" Round (about the size of a pool ball)
  • 2" Round (very popular size, probably what you have now)
  • 1-7/8" Round (small, like the old muscle car knobs from the 60's)
  • Small Taper (most people use this on a column shift)
  • Teardrop (you'd use this on a column shift)
  • Tall Oval (similar to the teardrop, for column shifters)
  • 2" Flared Top (check out the photos page for the shape)
  • 2" Oval (Old timey flavor to this one)
  • Other custom shapes available for added charge.

4) How much will it cost?

Prices are as follows; add $5 to cover shipping (by the way, if you order two knobs, I only charge you one shipping charge!):

If I put something in it for you, it's $25 a knob.

If you want a swirl pattern, it's $20 (easier to make).

If you want it lighted, add $10.

Optional brushed aluminum jam nut, $10.

Optional polished jam nut, $15.

Included in those prices will be the necessary adapter to fit your vehicle. I make some little extras, too, like special adapters that take extra time to make (no buttons!). Ask about prices, because it depends on how much time they take to make. You can get knobs cheaper than these, but you'll end up with "universal" fit, which usually doesn't. You'll also love the "feel" of these, smooth and comfortable in all seasons and temps.

Column Shifters

For column shifters (usually a splined shaft), a brushed aluminum adapter is included. Add $5.00 for polished finish. This adapter should be glued on with a good epoxy, like JB Weld. It won't vibrate or come loose. I can do set screws if you want, but they wouldn't be my first choice.

5) How do I pay for this?

I accept personal checks or money orders (payable to Bruce Roosa) when you place your order.

6) When will I get the product?

It usually takes about 3 days to make the product once I have your order. Another 3 or 4 for the Post Office to do its thing, and you've got a Koolknobs brand product on your vehicle. But just in case I'm backed up on orders, give me two weeks before you get to wondering about where your money went!
