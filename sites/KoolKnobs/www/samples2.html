Click on an image to see a larger view. Of course, these are samples;
with a little imagination, you'll have a truly unique project.

These glitter knobs are similar to the knobs popular with truck drivers. They're available in a variety of glitter and background colors.

This is the flared top shape in a blue and white swirl. The shape will remind you of a knob from a late model pickup. Comfortable, 2" diameter with room for pins and other fairly flat items.

This swirl is actually a mix of ivory and clear. The depth is awesome! Clear or pearled clear makes a great shift knob with just about any solid color (except black or dark blue).

Fishing flies make great knobs, with different background colors to set them off. This one went to Alaska; it's an "egg-sucking leech" with a color matched silver background. Awesome knob!

This is a hat pin for a '53 Buick knob, small taper for a column shift. Another satisfied customer of Koolknobs brand products.

New to the lineup, these tapered knobs are designed for column shifters (no buttons), but could be used on a floor shift as well. They're available in three lengths; 4-1/2", 3-1/2", and 2-1/2". Their largest diameter is about 1-1/4". A very classy addition to a steering column type shift lever.

This blue and black swirled knob had a very unique swirl pattern. Every one comes out differently.

Pearl, mixed with silver acrylic enamel auto paint, an experiment that worked really well!

This oval shaped knob is new to the Koolknobs brand line-up. Designed for column shift levers, items can be embedded that are under 3/4" wide or 1" long, like the Impala hat pin shown. The knob on the left was a small console knob.

This pinball shooter knob is an example of other uses for Koolknobs Brand products. Cabinet door pulls, drawer pulls, and other small knobs are also available.

This is a campaign pin, a real antique from the '50's. Food for thought. . .

Now available!!! Your shifter knob can match your vehicle paint color! Just send a 1/4 oz. sample of liquid paint to be used as the dye, giving you a perfectly matched knob to the color of your car, its interior, or other color you'd like.
