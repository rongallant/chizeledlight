Judo is a martial art that is more than a martial art. It is also a sport that takes in all the aspects of total fitness, including:

    Aerobic fitness
    Strength and flexibility
    Enhance the quality and quantity of life
    Confidence
    Patience
    Sportsmanship
    Respect
    And how to win or lose.

In Canada, Judo is the only martial art that has qualified coaches, certified by Sport Canada's National Coaching Certification Program, who can coach developmental and elite athletes.
