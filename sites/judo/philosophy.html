The philosophy of the Kokoro Budokai is to achieve, through judo, the goals and needs of its members. If the need is there we will try to accommodate.

The Kokoro Budokai exists to help people become good citizens, defend themselves, become physically and mentally fit, to provide judo activities for young and old, taking in to consideration their physical, mental and emotional capabilities.
