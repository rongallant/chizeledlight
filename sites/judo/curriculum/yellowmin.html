Yellow Belt Curriculum

Minor / Juvenile


Etiquette

    Care for the judo gi (judo uniform)
    Tie the belt (obi)
    Bowing: standing and kneeling positions (Rei)
    Respect and courtesy for senior ranks.
Judo Knowledge.

    Who is your sensei?
    Who is the head sensei of your club?
    What are the judo belt colours from White belt to Brown Belt?
Throwing moves I have to learn.

    O goshi (hip throw)
    Uki goshi (twisting hip thow)
    Morote seoinage (two-hand shoulder throw)
    Kibisu gaeshi (ankle pull-down throw)
Holding moves I have to learn.

    Kesa gatame (shoulder and neck hold-down)
    Kesa gatame gaeshi (how to get out of kesa gatame)
    Kuzure kesa gatame (two shoulder hold-down)
    Kuzure kesa gatame gaeshi (how to get out of Kuzure kesa gatame)
How to break balance.

    The eight points of balance.
How to fall so I don't hurt myself.

    Kneeling falls: Zempo kaiten or forward roll - Left and right.
    Squatting falls: forward roll - Left and right.
    Standing falls: Zempo kaiten or forward roll - Left and right.
    Standing falls: holding your partners uniform by the collar.

    Hiza = knee
    Kata = shoulder
    Koshi = hip
    Kubi = neck
    Mae = front
    Obi = belt
