Yellow/Orange Belt Curriculum

Minor / Juvenile


Etiquette

    Care for the judo gi (judo uniform)
    Tie the belt (obi)
    Bowing: standing and kneeling positions (Rei)
    Respect and courtesy for senior ranks.
Judo knowledge.

    What are the judo belt colours for seniors, from Yellow Belt to 10th Dan Black Belt?
Throwing moves I have to learn.

    O uchi gari (major inner reaping throw)
    Ko uchi gari (minor inner reaping throw)
    Ko soto gari (minor outer reaping throw)
    Ko soto gake (minor outer hooking throw)
Holding moves I have to learn.

    Kuzure kami shiho gatame (hold down the shoulders)
    Kuzure kami shiho gatame gaeshi (counter to Kuzure kami shiho gatame)
    Ushiro kesa gatame (reverse hold-down)
    Ushiro kesa gatame gaeshi (counter to Ushiro kesa gatame)
    Yoko shiho gatame (holding down you partner from the side)
How to hold my partners uniform so they won't get away.

    Migi shizentai (Right natural grip)
    Hidari shizentai (Left natural grip)
How to fall so I don't hurt myself.

    Standing falls: Zempo kaiten or forward rolls - left and right.
    Standing falls: Koho kaiten or backward rolls - left and right..
    Standing falls: Zempo kaiten or forward rolls - left and right over a partner's arm.
    Standing falls: Koho kaiten or forward rolls - left and right over a partner's belt.
Words I have to know.

    Hajime = begin
    Soremade = we are finished, or the match is ended
    Matte = wait
    Kansetsu = joint
    Kuzure = modified
    Mune = chest
    Te = hand
    Ushiro = rear
    Waza = technique or move
