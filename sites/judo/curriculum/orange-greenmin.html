Orange/Green Belt Curriculum

Minor / Juvenile


Judo history

    How did ju jutsu become judo?
    Is judo a sport or a martial art?
What I need to know about refereeing a tournament.

    How the referee team bows onto the mat?
    How to award the scores?
    How to award the penalties?
Throwing moves I have to learn.

    Harai goshi (sweeping leg throw. Remember to keep you leg straight)
    Harai goshi gaeshi (counter throw to harai goshi)
    Koshi guruma (hip wheel throw)
    Hane goshi (springing hip throw)
    Hane goshi gaeshi (counter throw to hane goshi)
    Morote gari (pushing throw by grabbing both legs)
    Tai otoshi (body drop throw)
Holding moves I have to learn.

    Kata osae gatame (counter holding technique to kuzure kami shiho gatame)
    Makura kesa gatame (pillow hold-down)
Joint locks I must learn.

    Ude hishigi juji gatame (arm-lock using the legs)
How to hold my partners uniform so they won't get away.

    Kenka yotsu counter and break (reverse grip)
    Back grip counter and break
How to fall so I don't hurt myself.

    Be able to fall from all throws at this level.
    Falls over a horse position from the standing position.
