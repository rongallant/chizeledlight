Throwing moves I have to learn.

    Sukui nage (scooping throw)
    Ura nage (rear sacrifice throw)
    Yoko gake (side hooking throw)
    Yoko wakare (side separation throw)
    Sumi otoshi (corner drop throw)
    Harai maki komi (sweeping, winding sacrifice throw)
    Uchimata makikomi (inner leg, winding sacrifice throw)
Neck locks I have to learn.

    Tsuri komi jime (thrust neck-lock)
    Sankaku jime (triangle neck-lock)
What are the principles of judo I must know.

    Nage no kata (Forms of throwing, Set number 3)
      Okuri ashi harai
      Sasae tsuri komi ashi
      Uchimata
    Katame no kata (Forms of grappling, Set number 3)
      Ude garami
      Ude hishigi juji gatame
      Ude hishigi ude gatame
      Ude hishigi hiza gatame
      Ashi garami
      There is at least one escape attempted with each technique.
      There are three attempts that you must learn.
There are combination and counter techniques that I must learn.

    Five combinations from all previous curricula.
    Five counters from all previous curricula.
How to fall so I don't hurt myself.

    Be able to fall from all throws at this level.
    Be better at all break-falls, than you were before.
