Orange Belt Curriculum

Junior / Senior


Throwing Techniques (Tachi waza)

    Koshi guruma – hip wheel throw
    Harai goshi – hip sweep
    Hari goshi gaeshi – counter to the hip sweep
    O uchi gari – Major inner reaping throw
    Ko uchi gari – Minor inner reaping throw
    Sasae tsuri komi ashi – Propping drawing ankle throw
    Tsuri goshi – Lifting hip throw
    Tai otoshi – Body drop throw
    Tsuri komi goshi – Lifting pulling hip throw
    Ko soto gari – Minor outer reaping throw
    Ippon seoinage – One-arm shoulder throw

Osaekomi waza (Holding techniques)

    Kuzure kami shiho gatame and counter
    Ushiro kesa gatame and counter

Shime waza (Strangulation/choking techniques)

    Nami juji jime
    Gyaku juji jime
    Kata juji jime

Break-fall Techniques (Ukemi waza)

    Break-fall over the belt (Left and right)
    Break-fall over a kneeling partner(Left and right)
    Break-fall over a standing partner who is bent at the waist (Left and right)

Judo Contest Terminology

    Hajime-begin
    Osaekomi – a hold-down is in progress
    Toketa – a hold-down is broken
    Matte- Stop the contest
    Soromade – The contest is over (That is all)
