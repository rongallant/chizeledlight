Green/Blue Belt Curriculum

Minor / Juvenile


Judo history

    How did judo start in Newfoundland and Labrador?
    Who are some famous Canadian judo coaches?
What I need to know about refereeing a tournament.

    You have to referee at three matches in you club?
    When do you insist that a judoka not compete any more that day?
Throwing moves I have to learn.

    Okuri ashi barai (double foot sweep)
    Tsubame gaeshi (counter throw to de ashi barai)
    Uki otoshi (floating drop throw)
    Soto maki komi (outer winding throw)
    Obi otoshi (belt drop throw)
    Sumi gaeshi (corner throw)
    O soto guruma (Major outer wheel throw)
    Utsuri goshi (switching hip throw)
    Tomoe nage (stomach throw)
Neck locks I have to learn.

    Hadaka jime (naked neck-lock)
    Okuri eri jime (sliding collar neck-lock)
    Kata ha jime (single wing neck-lock)
What are the principles of judo I must know.

    Katame no kata (Forms of grappling, Set 1)
      Kuzure kesa gatame (broken scarf hold)
      Kata gatame (shoulder-locking hold)
      Kami shiho gatame (upper four-quarter hold)
      Yoko shiho gatame (side four-quarter hold)
      Kuzure kami shiho gatame (broken upper four-quarter hold)
    Each of the five holding techniques has three escapes. You have to know these, and why they are used.
How to fall so I don't hurt myself.

    Be able to fall from all throws at this level.
    Be better at all break-falls, than you were before.
