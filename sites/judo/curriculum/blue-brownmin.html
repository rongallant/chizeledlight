Throwing moves I have to learn.

    Hiki komi gaeshi (pull down reverse throw)
    Yoko otoshi (side drop sacrifice throw)
    Daki wakare (reverse winding throw)
Neck locks I have to learn.

    Koshi jime (hip neck-lock)
What are the principles of judo I must know.

    Katame no kata (Forms of grappling, on the ground: Set number 2)
      Kata juji jime
      Hadaka jime
      Okuri eri jime
      Kata ha jime
      Gyaku juji jime
    There is at least one escape attempted with each technique.
    There are three attempts that you must learn.
What arm-lock techniques I have to know.

    Ude garami (arm entanglement)
    Ude hishigi juji gatame (arm-lock using both legs)
    Ude hishigi ude gatame (cross arm-lock)
    Ude hishigi hiza gatame (Arm-lock using the knee)
There are combination and counter techniques that I must learn.

    Three combinations from all previous curricula.
    Three counters from all previous curricula.
How to fall so I don't hurt myself.

    Be able to fall from all throws at this level.
    Be better at all break-falls, than you were before.
Words I must know.

    Randori = free practise
    Tachi waza = standing judo techniques
    Ne waza = groung grappling
    Kansetsu waza = arm-lock techniques
    Shime waza = choking or strangulation techniques
    Atemi waza = striking or hitting techniques
    Katame waza = holding techniques
    Kata = forms that show the principles of judo
    Shiai = a contest
    Uchikomi = repeated practice without throwing
