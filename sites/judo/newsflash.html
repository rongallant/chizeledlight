3M COACHING
Local coach up for award

St.john's judo coach Tom Gallant is among 24 coaches short - listed for this years 3M Coaching Canada Awards.

A male and female winner, along with runners-up, will receive $1,000 and $500 respectively at the Investors Group National Coaches seminar in Ottawa on October 30.

Gallant has been coaching for 32 years, teaching both recreational and competitive judo to students from ages 6 to 70.

He has Coaches at the club level, has been a provincial coach and has represented his province as head coach at the Canada Winter Games.

His athletes have won 5 national medals, over 50 medals at Atlantic competitions, and numerous provincial and regional honors.

Gallant is a member of the Newfoundland and Labrador judo Hall of Fame, and has been nominated for the provincial Sports Hall of Fame.

"The Evening Telegram," Saturday, September 19, 1998. page 32
