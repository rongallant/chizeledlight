The St. John's School of Judo teaches young and old.  Our youngest student is six years old and we have three in their fifties, one a kidney transplant recipient.

There are classes for kids ranging in age from 8-11 years of age, called minors; youth ranging in age from 12 - 15, called juveniles; young adults ranging in age from 16 - 18 years of age, called juniors; and adults ranging in age from 19 onward. These age divisions, for the most part are separated according to gender.

There is an advanced class where advanced kata and self defense are taught.

Self defense classes are also taught for women.

A judo program at the 880 Air Cadet Squadron, which is sponsored by the Knights of Columbus.

A six week introductary program at IJ Samson Junior High School.
