Kata Training

Back

    Kata is a series of pre-programed movements that teach the principals of judo, including attack and defence.

    Kata is the essence of judo training.  In Kata training you learn the basics, the theory and the true meaning of judo.

We teach the following kata:
    Nage no kata (Forms of throwing)
    Nage ura no kata (Forms of counter throwing)
    Katame no kata (Forms of grappling)
    Ju no kata (Forms of gentleness and flexibility)
    Kodokan goshin jutsu (Forms of modern self defense)
    Kime no kata (Forms of ancient self defence)
    Kime shiki no kata (Forms of self defence for women)
