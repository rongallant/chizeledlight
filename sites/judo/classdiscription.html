Class Discription



Minor classes
6 to 11 years 		Back

This group involves the teaching of judo games and judo skills in a relaxed atmosphere. The classes are thought by qualified instructors who have the welfare of the kids in mind.

    Respect
    Citizenship
    Fair play
    Social activities
    Sportsmanship
    fitness
    Competition - If they choose (emphases on fun)

    Benefits

    Better adjusted kids
    Higher grades in school (Fit kids perform about 10% higher in school)
    Healthier kids
    Self defence
    Rules of behavior - With respect to confrontation that work
    Fun




Teen classes
Ages 12 to 18 years 		Back

This group is the teaching of specific judo skills , games and social activities outside judo.

These classes are taught by qualified instructors who have the welfare of their students in mind.  In these classes, teens learn all the aspects that kids at the younger ages learn, however, they are adapted to fit the older age.  in addition, they also engage in:

    Competition
    Learn the rules of the game
    Learn how to win and loose in a fair environment
    Dedication
    Hard work
    Fair play
    Sportsmanship
    Commitment

    Benefits

    A place to belong
    Higher grades
    Travel
    Overall fitness
    Respect
